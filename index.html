Welcome to the website of the Rensselaer Rocket Society! We are premier rocket club on campus at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. In the past we have competed in such High Powered Rocketry Competitions as the NASA UniversityStudent Launch Initiative (USLI) Competition. Currently we are developing a rocket for the Intercollegiate Rocket Engineering Competition (IREC).
